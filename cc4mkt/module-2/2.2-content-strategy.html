Step 1: Research & Foundation

Create a comprehensive content strategy foundation for FocusFlow:

PHASE 1 - Audience Research
- Analyze our 3 personas (from brand/personas/)
- Identify content topics each persona cares about
- Map persona questions and pain points
- Determine where each persona seeks information

PHASE 2 - Competitive Content Analysis
- Audit top 5 competitors' content
- Identify content gaps we can fill
- Find their most successful content themes
- Analyze their content frequency and channels

PHASE 3 - Keyword Research
- Research 50 keywords related to remote team productivity
- Group keywords by topic clusters
- Identify high-value, low-competition opportunities
- Map keywords to buyer journey stages

Save all research in research/content-strategy-foundation/

Content Calendar Best Practices

Effective Calendar Structure

  • Theme weeks: Group related content by week
  • Persona rotation: Balance content across personas
  • Funnel coverage: Mix top, middle, bottom of funnel
  • Format variety: Don't do the same thing every day
  • Flexibility buffer: Leave 20% unplanned for timely topics
  • Repurposing built-in: Plan how content will be reused

Content Gap Analysis

Identify content gaps and opportunities:

COMPETITIVE GAPS:
- Topics competitors cover that we don't
- Keywords they rank for that we should target
- Content formats they use successfully

AUDIENCE GAPS:
- Questions we haven't answered
- Pain points not addressed
- Personas underserved

FUNNEL GAPS:
- Stages with insufficient content
- Conversion points lacking support content
- Objections not addressed

SEO GAPS:
- High-value keywords we're missing
- Topic clusters not fully developed
- Low-competition opportunities
