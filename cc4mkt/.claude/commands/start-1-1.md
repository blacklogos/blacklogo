# Start Lesson 1.1: Welcome to MarketFlow

Welcome to Module 1! Now that you have Claude Code installed, let's dive into your first real marketing workflows.

## This Lesson: Understanding Your Marketing Project

In this 20-minute lesson, you'll explore the MarketFlow project structure and understand how to organize marketing work with Claude Code.

## The MarketFlow Project Context

**Product**: FocusFlow 2.0 - A productivity app for remote teams
**Launch Date**: Q2 2024
**Budget**: $50K marketing budget
**Your Team**: Small marketing team (you + 2 others)

### Meet Your Target Personas

**1. Startup Sam (Founder)**
- Age: 28, runs a 10-person startup
- Pain: Team scattered across tools, losing focus
- Goal: One platform to manage team productivity
- Decision style: Fast, tries new tools constantly

**2. Manager Maria (Team Lead)**
- Age: 38, manages remote team of 15
- Pain: Can't see what her team is working on
- Goal: Better visibility and accountability
- Decision style: Careful, needs proof it works

**3. Solo Steve (Freelancer/Consultant)**
- Age: 32, manages multiple clients
- Pain: Juggling too many projects and deadlines
- Goal: Stay organized without complexity
- Decision style: Budget-conscious, seeks simplicity

## Let's Explore Your Workspace

I'll help you navigate the MarketFlow project structure.

1. **Brand Guidelines** - See the FocusFlow brand voice, colors, messaging
