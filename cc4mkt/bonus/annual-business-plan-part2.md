## Budget Justification Framework

### Building Your Business Case

This framework helps you justify your marketing budget to executives and secure buy-in.

---

### 1. Link Marketing to Revenue

**Formula:**
```
Marketing Budget = (Revenue Goal Ã— CAC) Ã· Average Deal Size
```

**Example:**
- Revenue Goal: $5M
- Customer Acquisition Cost: $10,000
- Average Deal Size: $75,000
- Required New Customers: $5M Ã· $75K = 67 customers
- **Marketing Budget Needed: 67 Ã— $10,000 = $670,000**

---

### 2. Benchmark Against Industry Standards

**B2B IT Services Benchmarks:**
- Marketing Budget: 5-8% of revenue (growth mode) or 3-5% (mature)
- CAC: 10-15% of first-year contract value
- CAC Payback Period: 6-12 months
- Marketing cost per SQL: $500-$1,500
- SQL to Opportunity conversion: 30-40%
- Opportunity to Close: 25-35%
